Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 April 2026 📋 Effective Date: 2 April 2026  ·  Applies to all MyRight users worldwide

MyRight ("we", "us", "our", "the Company") operates the MyRight platform — a web application and progressive web app (PWA) accessible at myright.app and related subdomains. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you access or use MyRight's services.

By using MyRight, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform immediately.

This policy is published in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 ("SPDI Rules"), and where applicable, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA).

1. Who We Are

MyRight is an AI-powered legal technology platform helping Indian users generate legal documents, understand their rights, and access legal information. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice. We are the "data fiduciary" in respect of your personal data under the DPDPA.

Contact: privacy@myright.app

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account registration: Full name and email address via Google Sign-In (password stored securely by Google — we never see it).
  • Profile information: Phone number and location (state/city), if you choose to add them.
  • Document data: Names, addresses, amounts, dates, and other details you enter when generating legal documents.
  • AI chat data: Messages you send to the AI Legal Assistant and Risk Analyzer.
  • Uploaded files: Documents (PDF, TXT, DOCX) you upload for analysis — processed in-session only, not permanently stored.
  • Payment information: Processed entirely by Razorpay. We only receive a transaction ID and payment status — we never access your card, UPI PIN, or bank details.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, documents generated — via Firebase Analytics.
  • Device information: Browser type, OS, device type, language settings.
  • IP address: For fraud prevention and approximate location.
  • Authentication tokens: Stored in localStorage to maintain your login session.

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect Aadhaar, PAN, or government identity numbers.
  • We do not use advertising tracking pixels or third-party marketing cookies.
  • We do not record audio or video.
  • We do not access contacts, camera, or microphone without explicit permission.

3.1 AI Processing

When you use the AI Legal Assistant or Risk Analyzer, your messages are transmitted to our AI service provider (Anthropic, Inc.) via a Cloudflare Worker for processing. Your conversation is used solely to generate your response and is not used to train AI models without explicit consent. We have a Data Processing Agreement with Anthropic.

⚠️ Caution: Do not share highly sensitive personal details (bank account numbers, Aadhaar, passwords, confidential trade secrets) in the AI chat.

4. Subscription Plans and Payments

MyRight operates on a subscription model with two paid tiers — Pro Monthly (₹199/month) and Pro Yearly (₹1499/year). Payments are processed by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled. You may cancel at any time from your Profile page. No refunds are provided for partially used billing periods except as required by applicable law.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Data stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) in Asia-Pacific data centres.
  • Encryption in transit: TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS) for all communications.
  • Encryption at rest: Firebase encrypts all data using AES-256.
  • Access control: Firestore Security Rules ensure users can only access their own data.
  • Third-party security: Google Firebase, Cloudflare, and Razorpay maintain SOC 2 Type II or equivalent certifications.
  • Breach notification: In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you within 72 hours of discovery.

6. Guest Mode

If you use MyRight without an account, no personally identifiable information is stored on our servers. Session data exists only in your browser's localStorage and is cleared when you close or sign out. We store only a non-identifiable flag (mr_guest=1) to maintain your guest session.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

  • Firebase auth token: Required for login sessions. Expires after 30 days of inactivity.
  • Guest session flag: mr_guest localStorage key for guest mode.
  • We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or social media pixels.

9. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We disclose your personal data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To our service providers listed above, bound by Data Processing Agreements.
  • Legal obligation — valid court order, subpoena, or direction from a competent Indian authority under the IT Act, 2000 or DPDPA, 2023.
  • To protect rights and safety — to prevent fraud, abuse, or imminent harm.
  • Business transfer — if MyRight is acquired or restructured, data may transfer to the acquiring entity under equivalent privacy protections with advance notice to you.

10. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active, plus 90 days after deletion.
  • Generated documents: Stored in your Vault until you delete them or your account.
  • AI chat history: Not persistently stored — exists only in browser memory during a session.
  • Payment records: Transaction IDs retained for 7 years per Indian financial regulations.
  • Analytics data: Anonymised and aggregated; retained up to 24 months.
  • Uploaded files: Processed in-memory only; not permanently stored.

11. Your Rights

Under the DPDPA, 2023 and applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate data via the Profile page or by contacting us.
  • Erasure: Request permanent deletion of your account and associated data (processed within 30 days).
  • Data portability: Request an export of your data in JSON/CSV format.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is consent-based, withdraw at any time.
  • Grievance redressal: File a complaint with our Grievance Officer (see below).

To exercise rights: privacy@myright.app. We respond within 30 days.

12. Children's Privacy

MyRight is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us at privacy@myright.app and we will delete that data promptly.

13. International Users

MyRight is designed for users in India and operates under Indian law. If you access the platform from outside India, you do so on your own initiative. Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate (including the United States). We ensure such transfers are governed by appropriate safeguards.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. For material changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent platform notice at least 15 days before the changes take effect. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
